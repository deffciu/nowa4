What is arbitrage?

Arbitrage is the simultaneous purchase and sale of an asset to profit from a difference in the price. It is a trade that profits by exploiting the price differences of indentical or similar financial instruments on different markets or in different forms. What is Staking?

Proof-of-Stake is a new methodology for achieving distributed consensus. It starts with storing your coins in your wallet. Once your coins reach maturity, you can encrypt your wallet (for staking) and start. Your wallet will start "competing" with other wallets for signing the next block. After signing a block you will get a reward (read below).

What is Mining?

Mining includes two functions, namely: adding transactions to the blockchain (securing and verifying) and also releasing new currency. Individual blocks added by miners contain a proof-of-work, or PoW. It basically needs a computer and a program, which helps miners compete with their peers in solving mathematical problems.
